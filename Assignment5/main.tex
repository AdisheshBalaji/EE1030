\textbf{Solution:}
The area under the curve is given by \\
\begin{align}
A &= 4 \int_0^4 b \sqrt{1 - \frac{x^2}{a^2}} \, dx \\
\implies &= 4 \int_0^4 3 \sqrt{1 - \frac{x^2}{16}} \, dx \\
A &= 12\pi
\end{align}
